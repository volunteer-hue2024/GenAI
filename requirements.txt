To run this complete agentic workforce in 2026, you need a precise set of libraries. As of early 2026, **CrewAI** and **LangChain** have optimized their integrations for Gemini 2.5 and 3.0 series models.

```text

crewai>=0.100.0
crewai-tools>=1.9.0

# --- LLM & Google Integration ---
langchain-google-genai>=4.2.0
google-genai>=1.0.0

# --- Environment & Utils ---
python-dotenv>=1.0.1
requests>=2.31.0

```
